What are we made of

The J.K.U.A.T. Society of Engineering Students aims at promoting engineering and cross disciplinary collaboration on campus through mentorship activities, engineering projects, community development activities, and professionalize collaboration, as well as serving as a resource for undergraduate engineering students at J.K.U.A.T.

Committees

Here are our committes

Publicity

We are the 'fun' part of the society. Handling the social media handles and making of posters, we get to engage the members of the society in their creative sides.

Editorial

We are a community of webdesigners ,writers and editors responsible for Ses website

Outreach

We organize mentorship events for primary and high school kids. It's divided into two: Outreach where kids are mentored at their locality and In reach where they come to our institution and get mentored.

Corporate affairs

We deal with the interactions between the society as a body and external bodies, such as companies and institutions, to achieve SES’ goal of developing wholesome engineering students.

Women in Tech

We are a 'safe space' where we can come and share our ideas when it comes to anything tech. Wit also exposes you to women who are already in the industry who give insight on life in the industry.

Projects

Our primary objective is to build a community that is able to foster collaboration and creativity and equipping members with the intended skills required to work on real world problems.

Mentorship

SES mentorship committee’s role is to share knowledge, be a support system and guide SES mentees achieve their objective, career growth.
